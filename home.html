Automatic Trash Bin

I conceived this project during the height of the Covid-19 pandemic with the aim of minimizing unnecessary physical contact and reducing the transmission rate of the disease. The project utilizes an Ultrasonic sensor to detect objects at a predetermined distance. Subsequently, it transmits signals to the Arduino UR3 board, which commands a Servo motor to operate, thereby opening and closing the garbage bin lid.
